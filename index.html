Frequently Asked Questions

1 What is an ecommerce seller?
e-commerce is simply the buying and selling of goods and services using the internet, when shopping online. However, the term is often used to describe all of a seller's efforts, when selling products directly to consumers.
2 What do you need to start selling online?
In simple word you need GST Number, Current Account and Atlest one seller account from these E-COMMERCE plateform Amazon, Flipkart, Snapdeal, eBay, PayTm, and Myntra etc...
3 do i need Laptop or computer for listing my product ?
No.. You can list your product just using with mobiles phone. You don't need for a Laptop or computer.
4 Do I need a current account to sell on online?
In Amazon, you don't need current account. but for Flipkart & other E-Commerce company you must need current account.
5 Can i sell without gst number?
If you are listing taxable goods, GST details are required to sell online. You need to provide GST number to E-Commerce company at the time of registration. However, if you are selling only GST exempted categories, then this may not be required.
